Poly Housing

Introduction

Welcome to Polyurethane Chemical Formulas, Inc., where we redefine modern housing with our cutting-edge polyurethane solutions.
Our polyurethane housing offers unparalleled benefits in cost, insulation, strength, design flexibility, and safety, making it the superior choice for your building needs.

Polyurethane Housing Advantages

  • Cost-Effective Building: Our monolithic panel system significantly reduces construction costs compared to traditional multi-part walls.
    This streamlined process not only saves on materials but also on labor and time.
  • Superior Insulation: Our panels boast an impressive R-value of 40, providing more than double the insulation efficiency of traditional 4-inch walls (20 R-value)
    and significantly higher than 6-inch walls (30 R-value). This enhanced insulation means increased energy savings and comfort in every season.
  • Enhanced Durability: With a PSI over 5000, our polyurethane panels surpass the strength of standard concrete housing, which typically holds a PSI of about 3500.
    This superior durability ensures a longer-lasting and safer structure.
  • Modular and Portable: Our housing systems are designed to be modular and portable, allowing for flexibility in assembly whether at our manufacturing plant or
    directly on-site. This adaptability makes our polyurethane housing ideal for a variety of locations and needs.
  • Customizable Designs: Thanks to our innovative use of mold plates, the design of both the exterior and interior of our panels can be changed in just a few hours.
    This allows for customization in square footage and interior layouts, catering to diverse architectural preferences and requirements.
  • Safety Features: Our closed-cell foam technology includes extinguishing properties that enhance safety. Our polyurethane foam is not only resistant to heat
    but also flame retardant, reducing the risk of fire spread compared to other materials.
