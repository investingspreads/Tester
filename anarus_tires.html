Anarus Polyurethane Tire Technology

Driving Towards a Greener Future

Introduction to Anarus and Tanarus Tires

Anarus, through its innovative Polyurethane Chemicals, Formulas, Inc.,
introduces Tanarus Tires—the world’s first truly "Green" tire. Engineered to revolutionize the automotiveindustry,
Tanarus Tires set a new standard for environmental responsibility and performance excellence.

Revolutionary Features of Tanarus Tire

Unmatched Quality and Sustainability:

  • 100% recyclable: Tanarus Tires are pioneers in sustainability, being completely recyclable and made from non-toxic materials.
  • Air/No-Air Technology: Innovative design allows these tires to function optimally, whether they are inflated or not, ensuring reliability and safety under various conditions.
  • Extended Lifespan: Designed to last twice as long as conventional tires, Tanarus offers remarkable durability.
  • Precision Engineering: Each tire is perfectly round and balanced, which enhances rolling, stopping, traction, and handling capabilities in all weather conditions.
