Innovative Technology Behind IGRIS

IGRIS: Inelastic Gamma Ray Imaging System (IGRIS):

The Inelastic Gamma Ray Imaging System (IGRIS) employs neutron scattering and time-of-flight
spectroscopy to identify substances. High-energy neutrons create unique "fingerprints" of gamma rays,
allowing precise identification of various elements.
