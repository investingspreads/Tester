Energy on Demand

Energy on Demand, Inc. has positioned itself as a forward-thinking company with the goal of providing renewable,
reliable, and affordable power globally. Their technology, inspired by Nikola Tesla, utilizes a revolutionary approach to generating electricity.
Here's a concise summary for energy on demand:

Company Vision

Our lofty goal is to supply the world with renewable, reliable, and affordable power.

Product Overview

  • Innovative Technology: The generators are based on a design that harvests electromagnetic energy using advanced materials,
    which enables continuous power generation through a low drag, high speed Electromagnetic Drive Engine.
  • Design Features: Unlike traditional generators, these do not use coils and are constructed from new materials that have a
    higher electrical conductivity and mitigate overheating issues.
  • Efficiency: Operates with greater than 99% efficiency.
  • Starting Power Sources: Can be started using conventional power sources such as diesel, natural gas, LNG, coal, or
    renewable sources like solar, wind, hydro, hydrogen, or battery power.

Advantages

  • Reduced Environmental Impact: Does not rely on fossil fuels for operation, aligning with global trends towards reducing
    carbon emissions.
  • Regulatory Compliance: Provides a solution amidst increasing regulatory pressure on fossil fuel-based power generation.
  • Scalability: Offers models that are scalable from small residential needs to large utility-scale facilities, with power output ranging
    from 50 kW to 1 GW.
  • Power Quality: Delivers a 50/60Hz Pure Sinewave, ensuring high-quality electricity is maintained.

Strategic Relevance

  • Market Positioning: Positioned as an alternative to traditional solar and wind solutions, meeting the growing demand
    for sustainable and efficient energy sources
  • Production: Utilizes mass production techniques, facilitating the broad distribution of various sized units tailored from individual
    homes to large-scale operations.

Energy on Demand, Inc. is shaping up to be a key player in the future of power generation by addressing the core needs of
modern energy consumers and industries with a blend of innovative technology and strategic production capabilities.
